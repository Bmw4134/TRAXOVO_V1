Information
What happens when you assign a driver?
  • The driver becomes the primary operator of this asset
  • The driver's attendance records will be linked to this asset
  • The asset will be marked as 'In Use' if available
  • The asset-driver relationship will be used in reporting
Before reassigning:

If you're reassigning an asset from one driver to another, make sure to update any physical paperwork and notify the relevant personnel.

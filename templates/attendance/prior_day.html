About Prior Day Reports

The prior day report analyzes yesterday's data ({{ report_date }}) for the full 24-hour period (12:01 AM - 11:59 PM) and identifies:

  • Late Start: Drivers who arrived at their job site after their scheduled start time
  • Early End: Drivers who left their job site before their scheduled end time
  • Not On Job: Drivers who were not at their assigned job site during working hours

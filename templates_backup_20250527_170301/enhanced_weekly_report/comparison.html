Processing Method Details
TRAXORA Processing
  • Input Files: DrivingHistory, ActivityDetail, and AssetsTimeOnSite CSVs
  • Field Mapping: Maps various column names (Driver, Contact, etc.) to standardized fields
  • Classification Logic:
    • Not On Job: No job site detected
    • Late Start: First seen after 7:30 AM
    • Early End: Last seen before 4:00 PM or <7 hours on site
    • On Time: All other cases
View Full TRAXORA Report
Alternative Processing
  • Input Files: Primarily telematics data (GPS/location data)
  • Field Mapping: Simple column name standardization without alternative mappings
  • Processing: Calculates hours based on start/end times without detailed classification
The alternative method focuses on duration calculation without detailed attendance classification.

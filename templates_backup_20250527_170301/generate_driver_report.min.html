Generate Driver Reports

Generate the daily driver reports using the latest Activity Detail and Driving History data.

Daily Driver Reports
This will generate the following reports:
  • Prior Day Report: Late Start, Early End, and Not On Job
  • Current Day Report: Late Start and Not On Job as of 8:30 AM

The reports will be saved to /reports/YYYY-MM-DD/ with the following formats:

  • prior_day_late_start.csv
  • prior_day_early_end.csv
  • prior_day_not_on_job.csv
  • current_day_late_start.csv
  • current_day_not_on_job.csv
  • report_summary.html
Source Files:
The reports will use the following files:
  • attached_assets/ActivityDetail (6).csv
  • attached_assets/DrivingHistory.csv
Information
Report Details

The daily driver reports include:

  • Late Start: Drivers who started after 8:45 AM (15 minutes after standard start time)
  • Early End: Drivers who ended before 4:45 PM (15 minutes before standard end time)
  • Not On Job: Drivers not at a recognized job site
